How to Use

Main

Bingo Master Board lets you host your own Bingo games.

Randomly draw Bingo balls by clicking Draw Ball or pressing the space bar.

You can also manually select Bingo balls by clicking on them.

Clear the master board by clicking Reset or pressing the r key.

Add ?masterboard to the end of the URL to instantly load the master board.

Bingo Master Board is not yet finished.

More features and better instructions will arrive in future updates.
